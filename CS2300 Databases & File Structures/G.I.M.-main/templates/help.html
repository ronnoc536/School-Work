FAQ

What the hell even is this anyway?

This is was originally the final project for the CS2300 course but this will serve as a way for TKE to manage all of the tools and equipment that we have in order to insure a good inventory and to keep track of important items.

What is the login and logout for?

The login and logout navigation items are for the house manager or site admins to go in and add and remove tools from the tke inventory database.

I want to checkout a tool. How do I do this?

Navigate to the checkout tool tab and enter in the requested information
